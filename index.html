HOW TO PLAY

MOVEMENT:

  • W/A/S/D or ARROW KEYS - Move character
  • LEFT CLICK - Click to move (point & click)
  • SPACEBAR - Jump

INTERACTION:

  • E or LEFT CLICK - Interact with objects
  • DRAG - Rotate items during inspection

INVENTORY & ITEMS:

  • TAB - Open/Close inventory
  • 1-9 - Quick select inventory slot
  • LEFT/RIGHT - Navigate inventory
  • ENTER - Use selected item

FLASHLIGHT:

  • F - Toggle flashlight on/off
  • TIP: Shine on walls to reveal hidden codes!

MENUS:

  • ESC - Open options menu / Close popups
  • M - Return to main menu

DEBUG (DEV):

  • ` (Backtick) - Toggle debug panel

GOAL:

Explore rooms, solve puzzles, and find clues to unlock the secret room. Use your flashlight to reveal hidden messages!
